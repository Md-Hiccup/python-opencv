Some Common Colors Represent as RGB tuples:

• Black: (0,0,0)
• White: (255,255,255)
• Red: (255,0,0)
• Green: (0,255,0)
• Blue: (0,0,255)
• Aqua: (0,255,255)
• Fuchsia: (255,0,255)
• Maroon: (128,0,0)
• Navy: (0,0,128)
• Olive: (128,128,0)
• Purple: (128,0,128)
• Teal: (0,128,128)
• Yellow: (255,255,0)
